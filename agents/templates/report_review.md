## Checklist

- Experiment overview is clear in the opening sections
- Abstract states the strongest result with numbers and scope
- Headline metrics are shown with case count, success / failure, and failure kind
- Each major claim cites a supporting figure or table
- Figures and tables are readable without surrounding prose
- Axis names, units, scale, legend, and caption are explicit
- Results and Discussion are separated
- Limitations and missing evidence are visible
